Create a modern, high-converting landing page for a startup called Followtize.

Product Concept

Followtize is a marketplace that connects startups with influencers/creators where creators promote products in exchange for equity or revenue share, instead of upfront payment.

The goal is to validate demand, so the page should be optimized for email capture and waitlist signup.

Design Style
Clean, premium, startup aesthetic (inspired by Stripe, Linear, Vercel)
Dark mode default
Subtle gradients, glassmorphism elements
Smooth animations and transitions
Use modern typography (Inter or similar)
Hero Section (VERY IMPORTANT)

Headline: Clear and bold, communicating the core idea

Example:
“Turn Attention Into Ownership”
or
“Creators Get Equity. Startups Get Distribution.”

Subheadline:
“Followtize connects founders with creators who promote products in exchange for equity or revenue share.”
CTA:
Primary: “Join Waitlist”
Secondary: “I’m a Founder” / “I’m a Creator”
Add a Three.js animation:
Visualize a network of nodes
Two types of nodes: “Startups” and “Creators”
Lines dynamically connect them (like matchmaking)
When nodes connect, subtle glow or pulse effect
Smooth camera movement / parallax on scroll
Section 2: Problem
Title: “Building is easy. Distribution is not.”
Content:
Founders struggle to get users
Creators rely only on brand deals
Use simple icons or illustrations
Section 3: Solution
Title: “A new way to collaborate”
3 steps:
Startups list their product + offer (equity/rev share)
Creators discover opportunities that fit their audience
Both grow together
Section 4: How It Works (Split for both sides)
For Founders
List your startup
Set your offer (equity or revenue share)
Get matched with relevant creators
For Creators
Browse opportunities
Promote products you believe in
Earn ownership, not just one-time fees
Section 5: Mock UI / Product Preview
Create mock cards showing:
Startup name
Category
Offer (e.g., “0.5% equity” or “15% rev share”)
Required audience (e.g., “Fintech creators, 50K+ followers”)
Also show a creator profile mock:
Niche
Followers
Engagement rate
Section 6: Social Proof (Placeholder)
Add fake/testimonial placeholders (to be replaced later)
Logos or “Coming soon” badges
Section 7: Final CTA
Strong repetition:
“Be early to the future of creator partnerships”
Email input + CTA button
Footer
Simple links: About, Contact, Terms, Privacy
Technical Requirements
Use React (Next.js preferred)
Tailwind CSS
Three.js for hero animation
Fully responsive
Fast loading (optimize animation performance)
Modular components
Extra Details

Use gradients for highlights (purple/blue tones)
Keep copy minimal but punchy
Focus on clarity over cleverness
Output
